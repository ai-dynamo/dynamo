## Notes

- **Stable** builds are QA-validated releases. **Nightly** builds track `main` and are experimental — see [Nightly Release Info](../reference/nightly-release-info.md).
- The selector covers the three most recent stable releases and the last 30 days of nightlies.
- TensorRT-LLM ships as a container only; there is no PyPI wheel.
- For the full artifact inventory (Helm charts, Rust crates, CUDA and EFA image variants), see [Release Artifacts](../reference/release-artifacts.md). For hardware, platform, and CUDA support, see the [Support Matrix](../reference/support-matrix.md).
