Choose your inference backend, a stable or nightly Dynamo build, and the backend version you need.
The selector gives you a complete, copy-pastable install command with the image tag or wheel
version filled in.

<InstallSelector />

## Notes

- **Stable** builds are QA-validated releases. **Nightly** builds track `main` and are experimental.
- The selector covers the three most recent stable releases and the last 30 days of published
  nightlies.
- TensorRT-LLM ships as a container only; there is no PyPI wheel.
- For the full artifact inventory, see [Release Artifacts](../reference/release-artifacts.mdx). For
  hardware, platform, backend, and CUDA support, see
  [Compatibility](../reference/compatibility.mdx).
